About the Apartment

This beautifully restored 147 m² duplex in Roda de Isábena offers an authentic yet modern stay. Featuring rustic wooden beams and breathtaking views, it’s the perfect retreat for families, couples, or solo travelers.

  • Two spacious bedrooms with double beds
  • Wood stove for cozy evenings
  • Fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances
  • Sloped ceilings and natural light
  • Beautiful views of the medieval village and countryside

About Roda de Isábena

Why Visit Roda de Isábena?

  • Spain’s smallest village with a cathedral, steeped in history and charm.
  • Breathtaking views of the Pyrenees for nature and hiking enthusiasts.
  • Cobblestone streets and preserved medieval architecture.
  • Authentic Spanish cuisine in local restaurants.

Roda de Isábena is perfect for those seeking nature, history, and tranquility. Discover this hidden gem today!
